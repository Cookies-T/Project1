My School

The school I attend is Cerritos College. The school was founded in 1955, and I have been attending for a few years now. The school itself has a nice atmosphere and is constantly finding ways to improve. There are many options to choose from, so finding the right class should not be an issue. Cerritos also makes sure that they have their education options be as affordable as possible, so anyone can be a student here.
